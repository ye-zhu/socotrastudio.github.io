Etsy Headquarters
117 Adams Street
Brooklyn, NY 11201

SOCOTRA STUDIO was selected from an open call to design and make the reception desk in the lobby of Etsy Headquarters. The layering on the desk was inspired by sedimentary rocks. The desk is made of reclaimed spruce, FSC beech, and has a Rubio Monocoat finish. The project is FSC approved and meets all green and sustainability standards.
