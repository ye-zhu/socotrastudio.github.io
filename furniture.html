SOCOTRA STUDIO takes the name from the Socotra Islands (an isolated land formation where over one third of its plant life is found nowhere else on Earth) as a metaphor for intimate growth, strangeness, and evolution as process. These traits are manifest in the uniqueness of the furniture we design and the custom furniture we build for our clients.
